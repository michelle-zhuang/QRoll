# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Attendees (dancers, club members) checking in on mobile devices quickly upon arrival at the venue. Admins (instructors, organizers) reviewing attendance matrices and managing rosters on laptops or tablets.

## Product Purpose
A lightweight, fast, and verified QR check-in tracker that verifies presence using GPS and IP fallback without interrupting the check-in experience. Success is frictionless check-ins and clean, legible attendance dashboards.

## Brand Personality
Energetic, dynamic, active. Emphasizes modern, active movement (fitting a dance studio vibe) but remains highly structured, professional, and reliable.

## Anti-references
Avoid dry, corporate administrative grids that look like traditional Excel sheets. Avoid loud, distracting gradients or micro-interactions that slow down the interface or make text hard to read.

## Design Principles
1. **Seamless Verification:** Keep presence checking completely silent and non-blocking for attendees to avoid checking-in delays.
2. **Dynamic Clarity:** Use color and light micro-motion to express system state (live, late, unverified) instead of static text blocks.
3. **Readable Density:** Present complex data grids with clean, airy typography and clear visual hierarchy to reduce cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA text contrast (minimum 4.5:1).
- Responsive mobile scaling for outdoor/on-the-go scanning conditions.
- Respect user preference for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
